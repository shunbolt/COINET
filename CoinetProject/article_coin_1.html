Chapter 1

Presentation of the cryptocurrencies


What is a cryptocurrency ?

For this course, we will work with a fictive cryptocurrency, the COINET. The way of working of the coinet is inspired from the most famous cryptocurrency, the bitcoin.

Imagine a Ledger, where one can write any transaction in order to keep a track of every transactions between a group of persons.

Then, Imagine that you are sharing your ledger with your roomates, what does protect you from their bad intentions? In fact it could be very easy for them to create false transactions from you to them...

When you own a Coinet, you do not really own anything. Nothing concrete at least unlike regular currencies as Euros, Dollars or yens.

Coinet, like every cryptocurrencies is fully digital, it is not controlled by anyone and like bitcoin, nobody knows and has to know who invented it.

During all this formation, we will try to make the cryptocurrencies technologies explicit and easy to understand.

Have a nice time with us and enjoy learning!
