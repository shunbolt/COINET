Chapter 1

Presentation of the cryptocurrencies

What is a cryptocurrency ?

For this course, we will work with a fictional currency, the COINET. The way of working of the coinet is inspired from the most famous cryptocurrency, the bitcoin.

Imagine a ledger, where one can register any transaction in order to keep track of every purchase between a group of person.

Then, imagine that you are sharing your ledger with your roommates, what protects you from their bad intentions? In fact it could be very easy for them to create false transactions from you to them...

When you own a Coinet, you do not really own anything. Nothing material at least unlike regular currencies like Euros, Dollars or Yens.

Coinet, just like every cryptocurrencies, is fully digital. It is not controlled by anyone and nobody knows and has to know who invented it. The volatility and unfamiliarity of the currency is why it is both fascinating but also feared by the pubic

During all this formation, we will try to make the cryptocurrencies technologies easier to comprehend.

Have a nice time with us and enjoy those lessons!
