How strong is a 256 bit security

As seen in the course, the concept of 256 bit security lays on the fact that there are a lot of combinations possible with a binary word of 256 bits. And by a lot we mean A LOT.

To be more precise, it is equal to 2^256 = 1,157920892.10^77. This number is so huge that it is nearly impossible to visualise.

Nevertheless we will try to imagine this number with some comparisons.


The energy needed to aliment a sufficiently large number of servers for a sufficiently long time in order to break a 256 bit key would cost a number of USD with 58 digits.

The quantity of energy necessary to break a 256 bit key is around the mass of the sun converted into pure energy.

Nevertheless, in the future years, with the arrival of quantum computers, the 256 bit security may will become obsolete and it will be time for us to find a new way to secure the internet. Some searchers are currently working on it but it seems that nowadays cryptography algorithms will remain unbreakable for a while.
