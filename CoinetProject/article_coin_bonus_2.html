The future of the blockchain


The blockchain became famous thanks to cryptocurrencies but a lot of people are interested in it fo others applications. Indeed, thanks to its amazing properties, the blockchain would be able to replace a trusted third party.

What could be the future fields of applications? Here are a few examples...
    banks
    notaries
    Uber like platforms (uber, airbnb, blablacar...)
    share of locally created energies
    keeping track on medical products
    land registers for emerging countries
Banks

Economists do not agree about the future of cryptocurrencies but a lot of them trust in the blockchain. Some of the greatests banks are currently working on it. R3 CEV is a company that is working with about 40 of the greatest banks in order to implement new financial technologies more details.

In France, the new Nobel Prize Jean Tirole does not trust in cryptocurrencies that he sees as "bubbles" but he trust in a future kind of virtual money, controlled by the central banks. The Crédit agricole, the BNP Paribas or Arkea are currently working on different applications of the blockchain in finance.

Notaries

The essence of the notaries is to be a trusted third party, the blockchain is totally able to replace a notary in a lot of his work. The fact that you can backdate a document or modify it in the blockchain for example is the kind of security that people want from a notary act.

Uber like platforms

It would be a kind of uberisation of uber. Nowadays, this type of applications are joining people together with an intermediate that takes a fee. With the blockchain it would be very easy to join the people with a peer to peer network without any superior instance.

Locally created energy

With the arrival of new energies that indivuduals can product, the challenge is to find a way of selling the energy produced between the individuals. SolarCoin is y cryptocurrency that actually works on this topic more details.

Keeping track on medical products

The fact that the blockchain is unique, distributed and transparent allows a amazing tool for tracking goods. One of the most important application that could be implemented is a system of tracking medicines in order to stop counterfeit medicines. In effect, counterfeit medicines are representing a growing threat in the whole world.

land registers for emerging countries

In a lot of developping countries, there are not land registers in many rural areas. There are no tenure security and people will have to implement a system. The blockchain seems to be a good idea in order to implement that system thanks to its transparency and security.
